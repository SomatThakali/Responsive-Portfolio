About Me

My name is Somat Thakali. Originally I am from Nepal, but Currently, I live in New York City since 2007. I did my undergraduate on Biomedical Engineer from The City College of New York. I worked in the Neural \research lab for 2 years. My job there was to design the algorithm to analyze the data generated from Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (tDCS) device. I was also a part of Backend developer team for a Burk Rehabilitation Center. I was responsible for creating and executing a fully functioning web application responsible for managing and storing patient demographic and neurorehabilitation data within in the application as well as exporting and importing data from an external database.
