About me

My name is Somat Thakali. I did my undergraduate on Biomedical Engineer from The City College of New York.
I worked in the Neural research lab for 2 years. My job there was to design the algorithm to analyze the data generated from Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (tDCS) device. I was also a part of Backend developer team for a Burk Rehabilitation Center. I was responsible for creating and executing a fully functioning web application responsible for managing and storing patient demographic and neurorehabilitation data.
